Amma appudu “Radha, ninnu kalavataniki Krishna vachadu choodamma. Edo matladali anta” antu keka vesindi. Radha vachi “Iddaram ala nadusthu matladukundam “ annadi. Iddaru nadusthunaru. Krishna emanalo thelika ila modalu pettadu, “Emaindi neeku? Are u fine? Phone lift cheyakapothey naaku ela thelusthundi?” ani adigadu. “Emi ledu edo pani valla super market lo ne untunnanu. Ee rendu rojulu koncham busyga untundi, nannaki sahayamga untunnanu” annadi. Krishna, “Ayithe enti vachaka oka phone cheyochuga? Sarele nuv phone cheyakapoyesariki ela unnavo ani malli ila vachanu.” “Vachinanduku thanks. Kaani nenu emi baadha padatam ledu. Idi naaku alavaate. Aa devudu nenu santhoshanga chudatam nachadu. Ee madhyane kastha nachuthundi anukunnanu kaani kaadu ani thelchadu” antu thana meeda vaali edchesindi. Appudu Krishna “I love you” ani antaadu. Radhaki oka peddha shock. 
“Asalu em antunnav nuvvu? Neekemaina picha? Inni rojulu manam manchi friends anukunnanu.” Ani thanani vadilesi vellipoyindi.

Ila oka rendu ellu gadichipoyayi. Radha inka Sheshuni pattinchukovatamledu. Krishna mathram thanani inka premisthune unnadu. Eppudaina thanaki kudirinantha varaku sahayapaduthuntaadu Radhaki. Sheshu thana girlfriend tho pelli ayipoyindi. Oka papa kuda. Radha vallu kudaa financialga baagunnaru.

Neeku repu sayantram pelli chupulu. Abbayiki nee photo chudagane nachesav ata. Neeku thanu repu nachithe ventane thambolalu thisukundam ani antunnaru. Nuv chaala adrushtavanturalivamma” anindi.
